## ICS/SCADA Security Basics
- Historically designed for reliability, not security.  
- Increasing exposure due to IT/OT convergence.  

## Common Threats
- Malware targeting control systems (e.g., Stuxnet).  
- Unauthorized remote access.  
- Misconfigured devices.  

## Best Practices
- Network segmentation.  
- Strong authentication.  
- Continuous monitoring & patch management.
